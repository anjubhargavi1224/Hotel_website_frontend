Room Types

Single Occupancy Room

A room assigned to one person. May have one or more beds. The roomsize or area of Single rooms are generally between 37 m2 to 45 m2

Family Room

A parlour of living room connected with one or more bedrooms. (A room with one or more bedrooms and a separate living space.) The room size or area of Suite rooms are generally between 70 m2 to 100 m2.

Duplex Room

The most expensive room provided by a hotel. Usually, only one Duplex suite is availabe in one single hotel property. Similar to the normal suites, a Duples suite always has one or more bedrooms and a living space with a stong emphasis on grand in-room decoration high-quality amenities and supplies, and tailor-made services (e.g. personal butler during the stay).
